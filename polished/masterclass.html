FAQ

1. What is the Admission Material?
A curated PDF with the essential theoretical foundation of the Black Silva method. Free to download and mandatory for passing the exam.

2. Is the Admission Exam free?
Yes. Theoretical knowledge should be accessible. You only pay for the practical, hands-on training with our Masters.

3. What happens if I fail the exam?
Review the Admission Material and try again. You cannot book Module I without a valid Admission Ticket.

4. Do I need to bring my own tools?
Not for Module I. For Modules II–IV we recommend your own equipment, but we can provide professional gear if needed.

5. Is the "Admission Ticket" a diploma?
No. It is your passport to enter the Academy. The official Master Diploma is awarded after Module IV.

6. How do I book the classes?
Once you have your Ticket, use the "Book First Meeting" button in Module I or our Fresha booking page.

7. Do I have to provide my own models?
Yes. The Academy is NOT responsible for finding models for the practical modules.

8. What if my model doesn't show up?
Without a model the practical session cannot proceed; it is cancelled and the fee is non-refundable.

9. How long does a module last?
Each practical module (II, III, IV) is an intensive 4-hour session.

10. Can I skip a module?
No. The curriculum is progressive — master one before advancing to the next.

11. What if I don't pass Module II in one session?
You repeat the module (at the standard price) until your technique is perfected.

12. Where is the Academy located?
Badstuestraede 16, 1209 København K, in the heart of Copenhagen.

13. Can I pay in installments?
Payments are made per module, so you progress at your own pace.

14. Is there an age limit?
You must be at least 16. Under 18 requires written parental consent.

15. What hair length should my model have for Module II?
At least 3–4 cm on top and enough on the sides to demonstrate a visible fade.

16. Can I bring a female model?
The current "Elite" course focuses on barbering and short-hair fading techniques.

17. What is the "1 Ticket" price for Module One?
Module One is free of charge but requires the Admission Ticket earned by passing the exam.

18. How can I contact the Academy for urgent matters?
SMS +45 52795671 or email blacksilvahd@gmail.com. We do not take calls during training hours.

19. Do I get a digital or physical diploma?
On completing Module IV you receive a physical Diploma and a digital version for your portfolio.

20. Can I reschedule a booked session?
Up to 48 hours before via Fresha. Late cancellations forfeit the session fee.
